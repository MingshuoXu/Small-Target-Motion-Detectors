08 March, 2024

Version Update: v2.0.1

Updates:

1. GitHub renaming the download package from "Small-Target-Motion-Detectors" to "Small-Target-Motion-Detectors-main" caused issues with addpath. In this update, I have addressed this problem by ensuring that addpath is not reliant on the name "Small-Target-Motion-Detectors". Instead, it now identifies "/matlab/smalltargetmotiondetectors/" to prevent addpath from being affected by GitHub's changes.

2. By changing the format of the demo dataset from TIFF to JPG, the size of the demodata has been reduced from 70MB to 12MB.
